# Future Capabilities

A **parking lot** for ideas that are explicitly **outside the active roadmap**
(\`ROADMAP.md\`). Nothing here is committed or scheduled. The purpose is to capture
promising directions without distorting current priorities.

> Rules: items here are not promises. Moving an item onto the roadmap requires a
> deliberate decision and consistency with \`LOOP_MASTER_BLUEPRINT.md\` and
> \`PLATFORM_CONSTITUTION.md\`.

## How to Use This Document

- Add ideas freely with a one-line rationale.
- Keep them grouped by theme.
- When an idea graduates, move it to \`ROADMAP.md\` and remove it here.

## Intelligence & AI

- **Network benchmarking** — privacy-preserving cross-tenant benchmarks (busy
  hours, conversion, no-show rates) so each business sees how it compares.
- **Predictive demand shaping** — forecast demand and proactively fill slots /
  reduce no-shows.
- **AI Employee marketplace** — shareable, vertical-tuned AI Employee templates.
- **Autonomous win-back** — AI Employees that proactively re-engage lapsed
  customers within policy.
- **Voice cloning per business** — branded org voice across all phone channels.
- **Multilingual employees** — automatic language detection and switching.

## Channels & Inbox

- **Google Business Messages / Apple Messages for Business** integration.
- **Unified social** — TikTok and other emerging DM channels.
- **Video appointments** — telehealth-style and real-estate walkthrough calls.

## Commerce & Operations

- **Dynamic pricing** — capability-driven, vertical-aware price optimization.
- **Inventory / supply awareness** — for restaurants and parts-based services.
- **Multi-location routing** — intelligent routing across an org's locations.
- **Field dispatch optimization** — routing and scheduling for home services.

## Platform & Ecosystem

- **Public API & webhooks** for third-party builders.
- **Capability/module marketplace** — community-built capabilities.
- **Partner / reseller platform** — agencies managing many organizations.
- **White-label** — EMG and partners reskinning the platform.
- **Workflow template library** — shareable automations per vertical.

## Data & Governance

- **Customer data portability** — export/import across providers.
- **Advanced compliance packs** — HIPAA, legal, finance-grade guardrails as
  installable capability bundles.
- **Consent & preference center** — per-customer channel/consent management.

## Notes

These ideas should remain consistent with the core principles: AI-first,
industry-agnostic, multi-tenant, provider-agnostic, event-driven, and
knowledge-grounded. Anything that would require forking the core or coupling to a
single vendor is a red flag and should be reworked before promotion.
